With a background in Civil and Architectural Engineering, I approach computational design as a bridge between innovative architectural expression and technical rigour. From large-scale architectural projects to research-led explorations, I have consistently worked alongside architects and designers, bringing an engineering mindset to creative challenges.

With a strong focus on building performance, I combine professional and academic experience in supporting design decisions towards net-zero strategies, using parametric tools to deliver sustainable solutions without compromising architectural intent.

Currently at IDOM Architecture as Architectural Engineer and Generative Design Leader, working on large-scale projects including stadium roofs, façade optimisation, and urban masterplanning.
